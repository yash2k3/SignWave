We are the creators of SignWave, a cutting-edge application designed to bridge the communication gap between the deaf and hard-of-hearing community and those who do not understand sign language. Our mission is to make communication more inclusive and accessible by converting sign language gestures into text in real-time. Using advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies, we strive to empower individuals and foster better understanding in everyday interactions, whether in personal, educational, or professional settings. Our team is dedicated to continuous innovation, ensuring that SignWave remains a valuable tool for enhancing communication across the globe.

1. Real-Time Sign Language Translation: Our primary service is the real-time conversion of sign language gestures into text, allowing for seamless communication between signers and non-signers. This service can be used in various settings, such as personal conversations, classrooms, or professional meetings.

2. Multi-Language Sign Support: We offer support for multiple sign languages, enabling users to translate gestures from different sign languages into text. This feature makes our application versatile and adaptable to diverse communities.

3. Offline Translation: SignWave provides offline translation capabilities, allowing users to communicate even in areas with limited or no internet access. Once the necessary models are downloaded, the app can perform translations directly on the device.

4. Accessibility Consulting: We provide consulting services for organizations seeking to improve accessibility for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. Our expertise can help businesses and institutions integrate tools like SignWave into their workflows to promote inclusivity.

5. User Feedback and Model Improvement: We offer users the opportunity to contribute to improving our translation models through feedback and gesture submission. By participating in our testing program, users help refine the accuracy and effectiveness of our services.

6. Educational Resources and Tutorials: We provide resources and tutorials to help users get the most out of SignWave. These resources include guides on using the app, understanding sign language basics, and tips for improving translation accuracy.

SignWave uses computer vision and machine learning to detect and recognize hand gestures from sign language. It translates these gestures into text that can be displayed on a screen, making communication more accessible.

Initially, SignWave supports American Sign Language (ASL), Indian Sign Language (ISL). Future updates will include more sign languages based on user demand.

The current version of SignWave focuses on converting sign language to text. However, future updates may include speech output as well, allowing for voice-based communication.

SignWave can work offline once the necessary models are downloaded. However, an internet connection may be required for updates or additional features like cloud-based translation improvements.

SignWave's accuracy depends on various factors, such as lighting conditions, camera quality, and the complexity of the gestures. The app is continuously learning and improving its accuracy through user feedback and model updates.
